WEB CODING PROJECTS



I am interested in extracting archival data from primary source materials into a tabular format, cleaning it and analzying it. During my time at Dollar Bank corporate archives I used ArcGIS, OpenRefine and Google Maps web services to reinterpret messy tabular data and visualize it onto a georeferenced map.
