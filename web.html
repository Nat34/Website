I am interested in extracting archival data from primary source materials into a tabular format, cleaning it and analyzing it. During my time at Dollar Bank corporate archives I used ArcGIS, OpenRefine and Google Maps web services to reinterpret messy tabular data and visualize it onto a georeferenced map.

Dollar Bank Data Map



I am interested in tools which support good research practice, assist with managing research data, and aid in the development of a data management plan. These tools can take the form of templates and web applications. The Digital Curation Center (DCC) and California Digital Library (CDL) have developed some very useful tools.

DMPTool Online (DCC)

DMPTool (CDL)



I am currently investigating search tools which leverage existing, free APIs. More to come!



Delivering web services and providing access to web content to people with disabilities is of great importance to me as an artist and information professional. I am striving to incorporate the essential components of accessibility into my web development and digital art practices.

To better serve those with disabilities I am taking W3C Web Accessibility Initiative recommendations for design, writing, and developing for web accessibility.
