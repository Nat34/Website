LIBRARIES

Libraries hold vast amounts of information and data. I am interested in helping people find, use and evaluate this information through reference and instruction but also contextualizng, identifying, accessing, parsing, automating, wrangling and analzing this data using programming technologies.

ARCHIVES

Archives embrace materials that lack a standardized structure. These materials come in all sizes, formats, and physical composition. I am interested in the digital tools which enable discovery, aggregration and dissemintation of these materials and their context.

LGBTQI

Libraries and archives have long been at the forefront of the equalities landscape, providing a place where people of diverse sexuality and gender can freely access resources and information. I am committed to serving the information needs of these individuals.
